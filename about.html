What is it?

Hello, dear user! We are happy to see you here.
Our site is a simple application what can help you to manage your work and keep all process in control. Here, in our site, you can see a list of your tasks. For ease of use all tasks are marked as active or completed.
On the main page you can see all of your tasks divided into two groups: Active and Completed. As you can see, you can easily define the current status of any task just by looking on its color. Also you can read detailed description of each task in your list just by clicking on its name.

You can see large images

On this page we use pictures to illustrate site's functionality. Some of them may be difficult to see due to its small sizes. For your convenience, we have added an additional function. If you want to see larger version of image with instructions, just click on it! To return to normal view, click on the large image and it will be hidden.

WARNING! This feature does not work on mobile devises! For using this function you must open this page on a device with screen width larger than 650px.

For mobile devices

You can use our site from your mobile devise with comfort. Its design will be adapted to your screen size without any problem! Just notice, that our site uses collapsed navigation for narrow screens.

Our mobile design has some differences between wide screen view. Check our site and find them all!
For example, the order of elements of "Task List" page will be rearranged on the wide screen.

How to open details

There are two different ways how you could open a task's page with detailed description of it.

  1. You can open it by clicking on the link "View Details" under the task's name in the list on the main page. This link will open a page with full information about this task.
  2. You can go to the detailed description from the page "Task List", where all tasks are presented. "Task List" can be opened by clicking on the appropriate button in the navigation bar or by clicking on the "Your Task List" on the home page.
